UnGen-Phi

An AI-Powered Username Generator

About UnGen-Phi

UnGen-Phi is an AI-powered username generator that leverages the power of the Phi-3.5-mini-instruct model. It dynamically generates unique usernames tailored to your preferences, whether you need something fantastical, futuristic, or just downright funny!

Key Features

  • Fantasy-themed usernames for RPGs and creative worlds.
  • Futuristic-style names perfect for tech or sci-fi use.
  • Funny, quirky usernames for casual and humorous settings.

How It Works

The generator uses AI to create names based on input parameters you provide. By selecting different themes, the AI dynamically adjusts its approach to come up with unique and fitting usernames. Whether you're building a fantasy character or need a techy alias, UnGen-Phi has you covered.

Technology Stack

UnGen-Phi utilizes the Llama model to power the dynamic username generation. Built for efficiency and flexibility, this tool is designed to run smoothly on local machines, taking full advantage of modern AI capabilities.
